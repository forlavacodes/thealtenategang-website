Our Fore Projects

The Alté Party

The Alté pool party was our very first event that took place during the summer 2023 at Cruzer Lounge and Bistro, Eleyele Road, Ibadan. It was an eclectic party with a massive turn out according to statistics ranging from 500 - 700 GenZ audience and youths from three major states in Nigeria; Abuja, Lagos and Ibadan.

The Alté'Native Fest '23

The Alté'Native Fest was an atmosphere that embraced individuality and creativity and connected with youthful energy in the Alté way. A celebration of alternative fashion, music, and art. It was an ideal way to find your own voice and connect with like minds through Alté music, art, fashion and your way of living as a conscious individual.
